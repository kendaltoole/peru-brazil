Day 9 – Week 2: Brazil May 10

Transfer to Jungle Lodge

Amazon Rainforest, Brazil

Overview

Theme: Arrival + First Taste of the Jungle

Today you make the transfer from Manaus to your jungle lodge. With a short transfer, you'll arrive with energy to spare for a gentle afternoon activity.

Transfer Details

Most lodges arrange pickup from your Manaus hotel. The transfer typically involves:

  • Van/car ride to a river dock
  • Boat ride to the lodge
  • Total time: under 2 hours (per your criteria)
The boat ride is part of the adventure! Your toddler will love being on the water. Keep snacks and sun protection handy.

Sample Day Flow

Morning Breakfast at Manaus hotel, meet lodge transfer
Late AM Transfer to lodge (boat ride!)
Midday Arrive at lodge, check in, orientation
Lunch First meal at the lodge
Afternoon Rest, nap, settle into your room
Late PM Optional: gentle sunset boat ride
Evening Dinner at the lodge, early bed

First Day Tips

  • Don't feel pressured to do activities right away—settling in is enough
  • The sunset boat ride (if offered) is usually gentle and great for kids
  • Apply bug spray liberally—mosquitoes are real in the Amazon
  • Drink lots of water—the humidity is intense
  • Let your toddler explore the lodge grounds (supervised, of course)
