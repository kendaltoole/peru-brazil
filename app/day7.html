Day 7 – Transition Day May 8

Fly to Brazil

Cusco → Manaus • Departing 5:10 PM

Peru → Brazil

Overview

Theme: Travel Day + Buffer Night in Manaus

Today you fly from Peru to Brazil, entering a completely different world: the Amazon rainforest. You'll overnight in Manaus to avoid a grueling same-day airport-to-lodge transfer.

Flight Details

Airline: Avianca

Reservation Code: CZEJJB

Fare Type: Flex

  • AV104: Cusco → Bogotá — Departs 5:10 PM, arrives 8:35 PM (3h 25m)
  • Layover: 2h 30m in Bogotá
  • AV081: Bogotá → Manaus — Departs 11:05 PM, arrives 3:05 AM +1 day (3h)
Climate Shift: You're going from cool, high-altitude Andes to hot, humid Amazon. Pack a change of lightweight clothes in your carry-on and be ready to strip layers on arrival.

Day 7 Game Plan

  • Morning: Relaxed start in Cusco—no rush
  • Early Afternoon: Final packing, check out of hotel
  • 3:00 PM: Head to Cusco airport
  • 5:10 PM: Depart Cusco
  • Late Night: Land in Manaus at 3:05 AM, head straight to hotel

Why Sleep in Manaus?

Many lodges require boat transfers that can take 1–3+ hours. Arriving late and immediately doing a long boat ride with a tired toddler is a recipe for meltdowns.

  • Break up the travel into two manageable days
  • Let everyone rest after the overnight flight
  • Start fresh for the lodge transfer tomorrow
Tomorrow: Transfer to your jungle lodge. Make sure you know the pickup time and meeting point—lodges typically arrange transport.
