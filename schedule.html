Week 1: Peru
Day1
May 2

Land in Cusco

Arrive at noon. Check into hotel near the historic center. Focus on hydration and resting to manage altitude.

May 3

Chill in Cusco

Gentle acclimation day. Plaza de Armas, local markets, stroller-friendly loops. Early bedtime.
Day3
May 4

Transfer to Ollantaytambo

Drive down to the Sacred Valley (~1.5–2 hrs). Settle in and explore the town at a relaxed pace.
Day4
May 5

Machu Picchu Day Trip

Train to Aguas Calientes, visit Machu Picchu, train back to Ollantaytambo. The big day.
Day5
May 6

Transfer Back to Cusco

Morning in Ollantaytambo, then drive back to Cusco. Quiet afternoon to decompress.
Day6
May 7

Chill Day in Cusco

Intentionally empty. Recover from Machu Picchu, let the kid play, catch up on sleep. Pack for Brazil.
Day7
May 8

Fly to Brazil

Cusco → Manaus (likely via Lima). Overnight in Manaus to break up the travel.

Day8

May 10
