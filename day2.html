Day 2 – Week 1: Peru May 3

Chill in Cusco

Cusco, Peru

Overview

Theme: Gentle Exploration + Continued Acclimation

Your second day is about enjoying Cusco at a toddler pace while continuing to acclimate. Keep activities short, flexible, and close to your hotel. No agenda pressure—just soak it in.

Kid-Friendly Ideas

  • Plaza de Armas – The main square is flat, open, and great for letting your kid run around
  • San Pedro Market – Colorful fruits, fresh juices, and lots to look at. Keep visits short to avoid overwhelm.
  • Stroller-friendly loops – The streets around the plaza are relatively flat and walkable
  • Chocolate Museum – Kid-friendly and includes tastings

Sample Day Flow

  • Morning: Leisurely breakfast, short walk to Plaza de Armas
  • Late Morning: Quick market visit or chocolate shop
  • Midday: Back to hotel for lunch and nap
  • Afternoon: If energy allows, another short outing
  • Evening: Early dinner, early bedtime (still adjusting!)
Remember: You're still acclimating. If anyone feels off, skip afternoon activities and rest. Tomorrow you head to the Sacred Valley at lower elevation, which will feel easier.
