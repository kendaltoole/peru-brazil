Day 8 – Week 2: Brazil May 9

Land in Manaus

Manaus, Brazil • Gateway to the Amazon

Overview

Theme: Arrive + Rest

You've made the jump from the Andes to the Amazon. Manaus is hot, humid, and a completely different vibe from Cusco. Today is about landing, getting to your hotel, and letting everyone adjust to the new climate.

Flight Logistics

  • Route: Cusco → Lima → Manaus (likely with a connection)
  • Direct flights from Cusco to Manaus are rare; plan for a Lima layover
  • Total travel time: roughly half a day depending on connections
Climate Shift: You're going from cool, high-altitude Andes to hot, humid Amazon. Have a change of lightweight clothes in your carry-on and be ready to shed layers on arrival.

Tonight in Manaus

  • Check into a hotel near the airport or in the city center
  • Grab dinner—Manaus has good food options
  • Don't try to do anything ambitious tonight
  • Hydrate—the humidity is intense
  • Early bedtime after a long travel day
