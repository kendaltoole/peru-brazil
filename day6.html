Day 6 – Week 1: Peru May 7

Chill Day in Cusco

"The secret to not over-traveling"

🧘

This day is intentionally unplanned.
That's the whole point.

Why This Day Matters

Buffer days are the key to successful family travel. They give you:

  • Recovery time after the Machu Picchu push
  • A chance to catch up on sleep
  • Flexibility to revisit something you loved in Cusco
  • Space for your toddler to just... be a toddler
  • Mental reset before flying to a completely different climate tomorrow

If You Want Ideas (But No Pressure)

  • Sleep in and have a lazy breakfast
  • Find a park or playground for your kid
  • Revisit the market or a favorite restaurant
  • Get a massage or spa treatment while partner watches the kid
  • Pick up last-minute souvenirs from Cusco
  • Just stay in and read/play/nap

Prep for Brazil

Tomorrow you fly to Manaus—a completely different world. Use today to get organized:

  • Climate shift: Cool, dry Andes → hot, humid Amazon. Repack accordingly.
  • Confirm flight times and any connection details
  • Charge devices, download entertainment for the flight
  • Make sure your bug spray and permethrin-treated gear are accessible
  • Review your lodge pickup details for Day 8
Tomorrow: You fly to Manaus. Have your carry-on packed with a change of lightweight clothes—you'll want to shed layers as soon as you land in the tropics.
